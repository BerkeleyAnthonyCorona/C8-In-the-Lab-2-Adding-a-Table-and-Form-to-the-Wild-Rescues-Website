About Us

Wild Rescues is a registered non-profit wildlife rescue and Rehabitlitation facility, located in Ocala, Florida. We help injured, sick, neglected, and orphaned wildlife. We help rehabitlitate these animals and then release them back into the wild if possible.

Wild Rescues is a member of the National Wildlife Rehabilitators Association

We help many animals, such as:

  • Racoons
  • Squirrels
  • Fox
  • Birds
  • Horses
  • Deer
  • Pigs
  • Reptiles

Did you find an injured or orphaned animal? Contact us to see if we can help.
